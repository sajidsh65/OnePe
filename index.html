Pakistan's SBP has set an ambitious national target to double digital transactions to 15 billion annually and digitize merchant payments through RAAST P2M. QR enabled merchants grew 111% in 2024, yet over 5 million merchants, kiryana stores, service providers, artisans still operate entirely in cash. They are invisible to the formal financial system. No credit history. No financing. No path to growth.

This is the gap we are building to close and we need a licensed banking partner to do it.

2
The Product
What One Pe Does

A merchant payments + Islamic financing platform for Pakistan's informal economy

We onboard small merchants with a QR code (no hardware, no fees) so they can accept digital payments instantly. Every transaction builds a financial identity. That data powers Shariah compliant financing offers, automatically, without paperwork.

📱

QR-Based Payment Acceptance

Merchants accept payments from any RAAST/bank wallet via a single QR. No MDR charges to merchants.

📊

Sales Analytics Dashboard

Merchants see their daily sales, trends, and customer patterns in local languages Options building financial literacy alongside digital habits.

☪️

Islamic Financing (Murabaha)

Based on average monthly sales, we generate financing offers structured as Murabaha or Ijarah. No riba. No collateral required.

Key Insight from BharatPe: In India, BharatPe reached 18M+ merchants by making payment acceptance free, then monetizing through merchant lending. Their loan repayment rate exceeded 96% because repayments are auto-deducted from daily QR settlements. We are replicating this model adapted for Pakistan's Islamic finance context and RAAST infrastructure.

How We Work Together
Three Partnership Structures
Option A
Payment Rails Partner
  • Bank provides RAAST/payment API access
  • We handle merchant onboarding & app
  • Bank receives settlement float & MDR from business accounts
  • Minimal bank involvement in operations
  • Best for: Banks wanting low-effort RAAST expansion
⭐ Recommended
Option B
Lending Partner
  • Bank provides payment rails + Islamic financing capital
  • We provide merchant data, underwriting model & app
  • Bank owns the financing product (Murabaha / Ijarah)
  • Revenue split on financing margin
  • Auto-deduction repayment via QR settlements
  • Best for: Islamic banks, Meezan, BankIslami, Al Baraka
Option C
White-Label Partner
  • Bank white-labels our platform under their brand
  • We build & operate the technology
  • Bank controls the merchant relationship
  • Technology fee + revenue share model
  • Best for: Banks wanting full brand ownership of merchant segment

De-Risking the Partnership
How We Protect the Bank
Credit Risk

AI Driven Underwriting on Real Sales Data

Financing eligibility is based entirely on verified transaction history on our platform not credit scores or paperwork. We only offer financing after a merchant has 90+ days of consistent sales data.

Repayment Risk

Auto Deduction from Daily Settlements

Inspired by BharatPe's 96% repayment rate: loan repayments are automatically deducted from daily QR settlement amounts before funds hit the merchant's account. No chasing required.

Regulatory Risk

SBP Aligned from Day One

We operate within RAAST P2M framework and SBP's EMI/fintech guidelines. All Islamic financing products structured with a certified Shariah board. Full KYC/AML compliance via NADRA integration.

Operational Risk

We Own the Merchant Relationship

The bank has zero operational overhead with merchants. We handle onboarding, support, app maintenance, and merchant education. The bank provides the license and capital rails we do the rest.
